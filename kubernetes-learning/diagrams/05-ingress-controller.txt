================================================================================
                    INGRESS CONTROLLER & ROUTING
================================================================================
Description: Ingress manages external HTTP/HTTPS access to services,
providing load balancing, SSL termination, and name-based virtual hosting.

Benefits of Ingress:
- Single external IP for multiple services
- SSL/TLS termination at one point
- Path and host-based routing
- Rate limiting, authentication (with annotations)
