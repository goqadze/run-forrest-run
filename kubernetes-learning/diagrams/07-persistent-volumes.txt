================================================================================
                    PERSISTENT VOLUMES & CLAIMS
================================================================================
Description: PersistentVolumes (PV) provide storage that survives pod restarts.
PersistentVolumeClaims (PVC) request storage from available PVs.

- RWO (ReadWriteOnce): Single node read/write
- ROX (ReadOnlyMany): Multiple nodes read-only
- RWX (ReadWriteMany): Multiple nodes read/write
